Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers

How do you verify tutors?

All tutors on TutoPlus undergo a rigorous verification process including identity verification, academic credential check, background verification, and an interview with our academic team.

What if I'm not satisfied with the tutor?

We offer a 100% replacement guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the tutor after the demo or initial classes, we'll provide a replacement tutor at no extra cost.

How much does tutoring cost?

Tutoring rates vary based on the student's class, subjects, and frequency of classes. After understanding your requirements, we provide transparent pricing with no hidden charges.

How soon can classes begin?

After you submit your requirements, we typically match you with a tutor within 48 hours. The free demo class can be scheduled at your convenience, and regular classes can begin immediately after your approval.

Do you provide online tutoring as well?

Yes, we offer both in-person home tutoring and online tutoring options. You can choose the format that works best for your child's learning style.

Can I change the class schedule if needed?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling. You can coordinate directly with your assigned tutor to reschedule classes as needed, with at least 24 hours' notice.
